# Membership Processes

Memberships are handled on the [BMS Membership Portal](https://portal.brisbanemaker.space).

To become a "member", you'll need to be on a membership tier.

1. [Register](https://portal.brisbanemaker.space/register) a new account.
2. In the portal, [select your membership plan](https://portal.brisbanemaker.space/account/membership-plan).
3. Pay the membership fees online.
4. Done!

Next, you should:

1. Complete inductions: [BMS Membership Induction (Canvas)](https://canvas.instructure.com/enroll/8P97NC), or [Login](https://canvas.instructure.com/login) to resume an induction (after you've enrolled in it).
2. Ask a volunteer or committee member for an access card. (Simply message a red name on Discord or create a forum post and someone will work out a time)
3. If you would like to be inducted for additional tools, ask a committee member or work group admin relevant to the tool you're looking for.

Please note that everyone who is able to induct is a volunteer, so we may not be able to suit your time; but, we'll try our best to.

Note: For automatic induction, please use the same email on Canvas as your portal account.
