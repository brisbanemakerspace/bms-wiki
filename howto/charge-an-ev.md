## Car

The Brisbane Makerspace **does not** have any EV-compatible charging spaces and we **strongly discourage** dangling a long extension cable connected to a power adapter located inside the workspace.

## Scooters and Bikes

Provided there is room in the space, you may charge your bike and scooter using any free wall socket.

We ask that you remain present in the space while it is charging.
