# Closing Up
If you're the last person, we ask that you close up the space and put everything back

1. Ensure all the chairs in the coworking area are under the desk.
2. Check the tables for any rubbish and put them into the trash can.

> Committee members will ensure that there is always a trash bag available.

3. Ensure all Windows are closed. There are only two accessible Windows; the one overlooking the carpark (next to the server) and the one next to the door overlooking the street.
4. Ensure toilet and meeting room lights are off.
5. Press "Off" on the omni-remote (white square on the wall next to the door).

## Manual Omni-remote Steps

If in case the remote is not working as expected:

1. Turn off the light switches (they can be found on the incove occupied by the black shelves (just follow the wall until you reach a corner).
2. Turn off the air-conditioners. Their remotes can typically be found nearby.
  - Streetside Aircon: The remote is usually on top of the black cabinet behind the rentable desks.
  - Carparkside Aircon: The remote is usually on top of the server rack (which is next to the window on the electronics bench.
3. Hit 'Pause' on the smart speaker (it is on the black shelf).
